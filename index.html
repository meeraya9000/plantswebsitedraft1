What are plants?

A plant is a living thing which grows from the earth, it is the kind exemplifies by trees, shrubs, herbs, grasses, ferns and mosses. It grows in a permanent site, it absorbs water and substances through its roots. It synthesies nutrients in its leaves by photosynthesis using the pigment chlorophyll. Plants can symbolise several meanings such as success, eternal peace and spiritual victory.
